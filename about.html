Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in LPG distribution

Building Trust Since 2002

Malik Brothers LPG was founded with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and affordable LPG solutions to homes and businesses across Pakistan.

What started as a small family business in Karachi has grown into one of the most trusted PSO distributors in the country. Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across Karachi and Islamabad, maintaining the same values and dedication that made us successful from day one.
